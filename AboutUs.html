About Us

We are India's One of the well Known and Finest Technical Institute

Terna Engineering College is a private college of engineering in Nerul, Navi Mumbai, Maharashtra, India, affiliated to the University of Mumbai. It was established in 1991 and received approval from the All India Council for Technical Education in 1994.It offers bachelor's and master's degrees in various engineering subjects.Terna Engineering College is approved by the AICTE and the Directorate of Technical Education in Maharashtra. Accredited with NAAC A grade, the College is affiliated to the University of Mumbai. Terna Engineering College has secured 40 position among the top private Engineering colleges in India by Times Ranking 2020.
